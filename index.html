Features

Your entire photoshoot. Four modules. One studio.

Build your own AI models, upload your garments, choose the location, and batch-export to e‑commerce — all without leaving Clad.

Models

Create and customize your own AI fashion models. Set body type, expression, pose direction and face angle — your permanent casting library.

Garments

Upload your product photos — front and back. Clad dresses your AI models with precise fit and drape control, level 0 to 4.

Location

Set the scene. Choose studio backdrops, outdoor environments or custom locations — the entire shoot happens inside Clad.

E‑commerce

Batch-export catalog-ready images from every angle. Go straight from studio to webshop — 100% production-ready output.
