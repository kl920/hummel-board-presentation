Cookie Policy

v1.0. Last updated: 15 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Drape Studio ("Drape Studio") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or interact with the Drape Studio platform (the "Services").

Similar technologies include local storage, web beacons, and pixels that perform similar tracking or personalisation functions.

This policy applies to drape.studio and all sub-domains. If any part of this Cookie Policy conflicts with Drape Studio's Privacy Policy, this Cookie Policy prevails for cookie-related matters.

This Cookie Policy applies to all visitors and users of Drape Studio's website and Services, regardless of subscription plan.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember your preferences, recognise your device, and improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies Drape Studio Uses

  • Essential cookies — core functions such as page navigation, authentication, and load balancing.
  • Analytics cookies — aggregated usage metrics that help Drape Studio understand how visitors use the site and improve performance.
  • Marketing cookies — cross-site tracking to deliver personalised ads and measure campaign effectiveness.

Cookie lifespans range from a single session to 24 months; exact durations are listed in the live declaration linked below.

3. Consent

Drape Studio manages cookie consent in compliance with the EU ePrivacy Directive, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and where applicable, the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR):

  • Essential cookies are exempt from consent under the ePrivacy Directive and load automatically based on Drape Studio's legitimate interest in operating the website (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Analytics and marketing cookies load only after your consent is recorded (legal basis: consent, GDPR Art 6(1)(a)).
  • You can update or withdraw consent at any time via the cookie settings.

In the EU/EEA, cookie use is governed primarily by the ePrivacy Directive, as implemented in national law (e.g. the Danish Executive Order on Cookies). Where cookies involve processing of personal data, Drape Studio relies on the GDPR for the appropriate legal basis.

4. How to Manage Cookies

  • Use the cookie banner shown when you first visit Drape Studio's site.
  • Adjust settings at any time in the cookie-preferences panel.
  • You can block or delete cookies using your browser settings. Please note that doing so may affect the functionality of certain parts of the site.

Do Not Track: Drape Studio's site currently does not respond to "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. You can still disable non-essential cookies through the cookie settings.

5. Cookie List

A detailed cookie declaration showing each cookie's name, purpose, provider, and lifespan will be made available.

6. Updates

Drape Studio reviews this policy at least annually and updates the "Last updated" date accordingly. Material changes will be communicated via the website. For how we process personal data, see our Privacy Policy.
